Sick Tips to Read While Sick in Bed (Devlog #3)

Jan 21, 2021

404: HEALTH NOT FOUND

Hey y’all! Thanks for stopping by. I’m sorry to report that there’s been no progress on Sinister Santa this week (which I can promise is infinitely more frustrating to me than you). My partner and I caught some kind of nasty cold around Monday and found out Wednesday our household is almost assuredly Covid-positive (thankfully everyone’s double vaxxed and asymptomatic so far). I don’t like to complain much when I get sick, as I’m extremely fortunate that it’s a rare occurrence and usually not all that bad, but this week was pretty miserable for our whole household. With any luck however, the worst should be behind us, and I can get back to my usual pace over the next few days.

UE4 BRILLIANCE FROM TWITTER

Since I’ve been too out of it to have an original train of thought this week, please enjoy this small pile of cool tips and tricks I’ve come across in my doomscrolling. If you like this stuff, definitely give these fine folks a follow, and follow me if you want your timeline filled with retweets of similar content.

First up, a fantastic idea from Joe on how to make mechanical tweaks easier for the designers and other non-programmers on the team (or just save yourself from touching your own weeks-old spaghetti):

https://twitter.com/joewintergreen/status/1480781360893554688?t=BZaV9LuNmc2-NV_3Zsi1ZQ&s=19

Unreal Directive saved me a good bit of headache by reminding us that Select nodes are a thing. I was working on the Naughty List (high score board) in Sinister Santa on Sunday and actually trying to remember this exact node but kept drawing a blank. I knew it started with an ‘S’, but Sequence and Switch nodes weren’t it. I felt pretty silly (though relieved) after seeing this:

https://twitter.com/UnrealDirective/status/1484022745520168960?t=6aS7Rkd2KV8V_3E0jAObZw&s=19

Finally, Let’s Make FX shared a simple but effective setup for generating noise in materials. I’d just grabbed one of the default noise textures included with the engine for the light noise on the snow texture, but I’d like to go back and play around with this instead, time permitting:

https://twitter.com/letsmakefx/status/1479286421038850048?t=vBMCF7XiUWOBSGc3P6iE8g&s=19

UPCOMING DYING MMO JAM

I want to give a shoutout to what might be the coolest game jam idea I’ve seen in a while. Salem of Fortresses (@duck_bubbles) is hosting the Dying MMO Jam on Itch.io from February 3rd to 20th. It’s a really intriguing concept with a very permissive ruleset. I plan on giving it a go (even with my abysmal track record of actually finishing game jams) and I’d love to see more folks sign up. Otherwise, keep an eye out for all the games that come out of this and the devs some love.

https://itch.io/jam/death-of-an-mmo-game-jam

That’s enough rambling for this week. I’m off to down a bunch of tea and cold meds. At least I’m bound to appreciate being able to breathe through my nose and look at a screen headache-free significantly more when I do get to working on the game again over the next week. Stay safe out there, play some cool games (and tell me about them if you do, I love finding out about new games!) and stay weird!
