Buckshot

A rogue prince seeks revenge on his former friends with only the power of his own wits and a demon-possessed shotgun.

  • Platform: Windows
  • Genre: 2D Action Platformer
  • Engine: GameMaker Studio 2
  • Role: Programmer

Buckshot was the first project for which I embraced taking a tech-focused approach to game design. Our team of five shared an ambitious vision for a fast-paced platformer that would push us to learn fast and iterate faster. Despite lacking formal training and having previously focused on level design, I stepped up to the role of programmer and used prior experience in GameMaker to help bring Buckshot to life.
