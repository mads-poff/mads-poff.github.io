Sinister Santa

Santa’s got a taste for some Ho Ho HOMICIDE!

  • Platform: Windows
  • Genre: Retro action shooter
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Role: Solo developer

Face endless hordes of holiday horrors in this 3D arcade style shoot ‘em up. Only the flimsy cover of your snow fort guards you from the ravenous legions of Santa’s army. Make ready your best snowball-throwing arm and prepare to pelt the pests with as many fastballs as you can muster.

Sinister Santa was born of a last minute urge to commemorate the end of 2021 with one final weird little game. What started as a quick experiment in a static shooter control scheme quickly snowballed into a a whimsical quest to answer unasked questions like “What if elves were murderously inclined?” and “How convincingly can I fake a PSX game?”

Working alone, I’ve spent roughly a month building the game from the ground up. Designs for the snowball combat stemmed from a mix of arcade inspirations. The chunky graphics and gritty goriness came from my ongoing passion for the PSX indie horror genre (including fantastic titles like No Players Online, Sanctified, and Hollow Woods).

Sinister Santa is set to release as donationware on Itch.io in February 2022. Follow me on Twitter and Itch.io at @mvpgamedev to stay up to date on the game’s development and release.
