Godslayer

The Sumerian First-Person Slaughterfest inspired by the tradition of retro shooters matched with modern design and aesthetics.

  • Platform: Windows
  • Genre: Retro-style arena shooter
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Role: Systems Designer, Blueprints Programmer

From the stars came the gods, and their gifts were plenty. But such splendor was to come at a price - serve or die. Become Masku, the demigod son of Enkidu, and must liberate the city-states of Sumer from invading reptillian Annunaki. Using magic stolen from the gods themselves, face off against the Annunaki horde and make it clear that humanity intends to forge its own destiny without them.

Prototyped from January to May of 2021, Godslayer is the culmination of years of fascination in ancient lore, conspiracy theories, and Quake-like retro shooters. As one part of a team of seven, my tasks included programming and iterating on the combat mechanics of Godslayer as well as meeting various other design and technical needs. Key systems I developed included the player character (movement, logical state machine, spellcasting), AI (design and implementation), and level scripting. I also managed feature and level optimization to ensure Godslayer hit a steady 60 fps on Windows PCs.

“Godslayer blends the action of a first person shooter with the magic of a deep RPG to produce killer gameplay! I really love the theme!“ - Jack Mamais, Co-Founder of Impeller Studios
